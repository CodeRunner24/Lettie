Introduction

Lettie ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

  • User Data: We collect information you provide when creating recommendation packets, including student names, achievements, academic information, and other details you enter into the app.
  • API Keys: If you choose to use our AI-powered features, you may provide API keys (such as Gemini API keys) which are stored securely on your device. We do not have access to your API keys.
  • App Usage Data: We may collect anonymous usage statistics to improve our services.
  • Device Information: Basic device information may be collected for app functionality and troubleshooting.
  • Subscription Information: If you purchase a subscription, payment information is processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not store your payment details.

How We Use Your Information

  • To generate recommendation letter packets and content
  • To improve our app's features and user experience
  • To provide customer support
  • To manage your subscription and provide premium features

Data Storage and Security

All data processing occurs locally on your device. Your recommendation packets, user data, and API keys are stored securely on your device. We do not transmit your personal data or recommendation packets to our servers. All packet generation and processing is performed using the API keys you provide, and we do not have access to your data or API keys.

Third-Party Services

Lettie uses third-party AI services (such as Google Gemini or other AI providers) through API keys that you provide. When you use these services, your data is sent to these third-party providers according to their privacy policies. We recommend reviewing the privacy policies of any AI service providers you choose to use.

We may use third-party services for analytics and app functionality. These services have their own privacy policies.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, modify, or delete your personal information. You can do this through the app settings or by contacting us. Since all data is stored locally on your device, you can delete the app at any time to remove all your data.

Children's Privacy

Lettie is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the Support page.
